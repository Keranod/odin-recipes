Papka

Description

Papka self-made dish(?) sweet and full of protein.

Very filling and healthy.

Ingredients

  • 450g low fat cottage cheese
  • 5g cinammon
  • 15g walnuts
  • 60g oats
  • 120g boiling water
  • 50g raisins
  • 100~200g custard of choice

Steps

  1. Put oats, raisins and walnuts into a bowl/deep plate
  2. Pour boiling water on the edges and then over remaining not wet oats
  3. Cover bowl/deep plate with flat plate and leave for 3~5 min
  4. Put cinammon, cottage cheese and custard
  5. Mix well and ready to eat
